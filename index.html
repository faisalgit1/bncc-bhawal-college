    1.Bhawal Badre Alam Govt. College.

    2.Gazipur Cantonment College.

    3.Rajendrapur Cantonment public high school and college.

    4.Vhasa Shaheed Abdul Jabber Ansar VDP School and College.

    5. Shahid Tajuddin Ahmad Degree college.

    6.Kaliganj RRN Pilot Govt High School.
